BOBA SHOPS IN CHARLOTTE


Binki Cafe

Near UNC Charlotte's campus, this boba shop is very popular among students. With it's trendy decor, it's variety of aesthetic tea and food options,it won Charlotte magazine's best boba award.


Boba Baba

Located in South End, Boba Baba, although opened up recently and small, the boba shop has become popular in a short amount of time. Particularly their mochi donuts have become one of their signature items.In addition, with their marble seating and "Wet Grass" sign makes the shop stand out.


Tea Fusion Uptown


Milk Cha Cha

Located past Uptown Charlotte, Milk Cha Cha is one of the boujeeest boba places you will ever go to. It's iconic aesthetic tea-riffic neon sign and other refined decor make it a very instagrammable boba shop.
