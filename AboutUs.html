About Us

Have you ever wanted to know where the nearest boba place was or where boba merch was sold. Yadhira and Safa decided to bring all these troubles into one blog where you could find anything. We both are big boba conniseurs and felt like there were not many blogs helping first time users find a boba place or what boba is. Going into a boba place can be a bit intimatding since there's so many options. This blog will help you get into this aesthetic lifestyle (purrr).
